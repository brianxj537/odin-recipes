Sloppy Joes

Description

Buy these ingredients and follow these steps to make yummy sloppy joes in your crock pot

Your husband will be so happy

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Ketchup
  • Hamburger buns - King's Hawaiian Recommended
  • Large Crock Pot
  • worchestier sauce
  • two 14 oz cans of tomato sauce
  • beef broth
  • garlic power, salt and pepper
  • vegetable oil

Steps

    Follow these steps in the order below
  1. heat pan on medium high heat with vegetable oil
  2. Add ground beef, use wooden spoon to mash up beef
  3. cook until browned approx 6-8 min
  4. put beef mixture in crock pot
  5. top with tomato sauce, worchestier, ketchup and seasonings
  6. cook on low for 7-8 hours
  7. add on toasted bun of choice
  8. extras includes: mayo, sliced pickles and shredded cheese
