Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Description

Pasta dish with garlic and parmesan cooked with noodles of your choice

Ingredients

  • pasta of your choice (we like rotini)
  • four cloves of garlic minced
  • one cup of milk
  • two cups of chicken broth
  • salt and pepper
  • half cup of grated parmesan
  • parsley for garnish
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Sautee garlic in olive oil with salt and pepper until fragrant, 1-2 min
  2. Add pasta, milk and chicken broth
  3. Stir and wait for boil
  4. Once boiling, turn heat to simmer, stir occasionally until water is absorbed and noodles are tender
  5. Once ready, stir in parmesan cheese (if too thick and more milk until desired consistency is achieved
  6. Top with parsley and enjoy
