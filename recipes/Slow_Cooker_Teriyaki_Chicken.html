Slow Cooker Teriyaki Chicken

Description

Slow cooker meal made in Crock pot with chicken over rice

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs of Chicken Breast
  • 2 cups of chopped bell peppers, your choice of color
  • 1 cup of teriyaki sauce (we like soyaki from Trader Joes
  • 1/3 cup of Chicken Broth
  • 1 tbsp of ginger

Steps

  1. Put everything in Crock Pot
  2. Turn Crock Pot on high setting for 4-5 hrs
  3. Serve with rice and vegetable of choice
  4. Enjoy!
